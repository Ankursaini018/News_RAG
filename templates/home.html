Pipeline

How News RAG works

From BBC articles to grounded answers — a clean retrieval loop you can trust.

  1. 01

    Ingest

    BBC news articles are collected, cleaned, and prepared for search.

  2. 02

    Embed

    Content is chunked and turned into vectors for semantic retrieval.

  3. 03

    Retrieve

    Your question finds the most relevant passages from the news index.

  4. 04

    Answer

    The model generates a clear response grounded in those sources.
